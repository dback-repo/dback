A volume mounted to multiple containers. We are must stop all containers that mount the volume, for backup volume.
Links in the volume (hard or symbol)
Permissions in the volume
If we want to backup containers, we must save also networks

CLI format

dback dobackup local
dback dobackup local someContainer
dback dobackup local someContainer:/var/data

dback dobackup remote 192.168.1.100
dback dobackup remote 192.168.1.100 someContainer

dback restore local
dback restore local someContainer
